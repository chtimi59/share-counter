Read Key is needed to get a record from your counter, yours is:

{{currentCounter.rkey}} Copy

Possible records fields are:

  • 'date': epoch
  • 'lat': latitude (WGS84)
  • 'lng': longitude (WGS84)
  • 'alt': altitude (in meter)
  • 'author': up to 40 unicode characters
  • 'text': up to 255 unicode characters
  • 'value': numeric value

Read latest record

Read only one specific field (here 'value')
